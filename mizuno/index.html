The Event

Each year the members of the Mizuno Golf Forum get together to thrash round a golf course and find out just who can backup their online chat with real world endeavour!

As long as you are a member of the forum you can come along. We have designed the weekend with some flexibility if you can't be there for the whole thing.

  • Team competition on day 1
  • Evening meal and entertainment
  • Overnight stay
  • Singles competition, with big prizes available!
